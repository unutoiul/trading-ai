PATTERN DEFINITIONS
- strong_up: BTC shows strong upward momentum with RSI > 60
- strong_down: BTC shows strong downward momentum with RSI < 40
- volatility_breakout: Sudden increase in BTC volatility with large price movement
- steady_climb: Sustained upward movement with lower volatility
- steady_decline: Sustained downward movement with lower volatility
- rsi_divergence: Price increases while RSI decreases
- macd_crossover: MACD line crosses above the signal line
- stoch_oversold_bounce: Stochastic oscillator bounces from oversold territory
- rsi_overbought: RSI exceeds 70, indicating overbought conditions
- rsi_oversold: RSI falls below 30, indicating oversold conditions
